ABOUT DAVIGNER

HI I AM RUFUS DAVID O, I'M A 19 YEAR-OLD DEVELOPER AND DESIGNER. I WAS BORN ON 2nd DECEMBER IN PORT-HARCOURT, NIGERIA. NG. I ALWAYS KNEW I WANTED TO BE A DEVELOPER SINCE THE AGE OF 12...

I HAD FALLEN IN LOVE WITH COMPUTERS AND DEVICES, AND MY PARENTS DID TAKE NOTICE.

BUT IN 2014, SOMETHING SPECIAL HAPPENED. I STARTED MAKING ANIMATION VIDEOS DESIGNING AND SKECTCHING ON ANY PAPER I SEE AT HOME KNOWING THAT I'M A BIT OF A STICKLER FOR DETAILS AND A PERFECTIONIST, I DESIGNED MY LOG BANNER AND VIDEO THUMBNAILS: THAT'S HOW I GOT INTO THE DESIGN BUSINESS.

AS MY DESIGN SKILLS GREW, I THOUGHT WHY NOT OFFER MY SKILLS TO OTHER STUDENTS IN HIGH SCHOOL I CREATED AN ORGANINSATION WITH A GROUP OF MY FRIENDS CALLED ADAM'S APPLE INC WHERE WE MAKE COMICS AND STORIES FOR STUDENTS, WHICH DID REALLY GOOD BECAUSE WE WENT VIRAL, WE HAD STUDENTS GIVE US THIER TUCSHOP MONEY JUST TO GET OUR COMICS AND READ OUR STORY,

BUT UNFORTUNATLEY I CHANGED MY SCHOOL AND BASED OF THE FACT I WAS THE WHOLE INSPIRATION ABOUT THE COMPANY, THE COMPANY FADED AWAY. SO MOVING ON I STOPPED DESIGN AND CREATING FOR A WHILE I JUST DO IT FOR FUN AND MY MOM DID NOTICE AND SHE KEPT ON ENCOURGAING ME STILL THAT I SHOULD NOT LET MY TALENT GO TO WASTE(TYPICAL MOM'S) YOU KNOWüòÅ

IN APRIL 2023, I DID A INTERNSHIP IN A WEB AGENCY. THIS AGENCY IS SPECIALIZED IN WEBSITE CREATION AND WEB DEVELOPMENT. THIS INTERNSHIP CONFIRMED MY CHOICE TO MAKE WEB DEVELOPMENT MY LIVELIHOOD. IT'S AT THIS PERIOD THAT I STARTED TO WORK ON UI DESIGN (IN ADDITION TO GRAPHIC DESIGN).
